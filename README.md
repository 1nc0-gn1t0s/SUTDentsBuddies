# SUTDentsBuddies


tg bot for sutd meetings (especially for those who just came to study here, cuz people always need to introduce themselves in the chats and it may be hard for them to communicate with new people (?)):


possible functions:



1) adjust my profile 
 	- date of birth 
	- sutd desired pillar
	- hobby
	- fifth rows
	- local / international (where from)
	- who am looking for
	- interests (favorite movies / books / games etc)
	- photos
	- fun facts
	- term
	- cohort classroom
	- living in hostel (which block) / not hostel
	- why sutd?

2) send my profile (can choose what exactly to send)

3) find matches (the bot sends you people matching with you the most)
	- can put a thumb up / down 

4) find people who... (write your own, embedding ai will find people needed)
